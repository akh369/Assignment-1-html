    About Us

    Founded in the late 1800's by a couple of goodhearted dairy farmers, Legion Dairy Farm has been a staple in the dairy business in Europe and has been satisying customers for more then two centuries. Born form a vision to only provide the highest quality milk and dairy products to its customers, Legion Dairy Farm has kept true to its humble origins and now is one of the industry leaders in all things dairy related. All dairy products from Legion Dairy Farm are guaranteed to be the freshes and the most nutritious dairy product money can buy.
