    Cow Milk Milked from the utters of a fully mature and healthy cow, our cow's milk are internationally graded as grade "S" by the Europeon Food Communites. The milk is pasteurized at an exact temperature where are the harmful bacteria are killed while all the nutrition remains.
    Cheese Formed by the coagulation of the milk of one of our own specially bred cows, our cheese are prepared the same exact way our founders did more then two centuries ago. This has allowed our cheese to be among one the most flavourful cheese in the world. No checimals or artifical ingredients added, only the most natural and organic ingredients are used.
    Butter Our Butter are made by churning fresh cow's milk to seperate the buttermilk. Unlike most modern dairy companies, we do not use clunky machines, rather we rely on human manpower as we think that each and every roll of butter we produce is unique in its own special way. Our butter contains an exact 80% of butterfat with no artifical ingredients
    Ice Cream An absolute frozen delight. Give your taste buds a treat with our newly launched ice cream made by using only the finest cow's milk and frozen to an absolute chilling temperature. No additional sugar or artifical flavouring added, yet every bit as sweet and nutritious.

Not to mention the high nutritious content of our yougurt, only a fool would pass down a bowl of one of our own Legion Yogurt.
    Sour Cream Enjoy even the simplest of treats with our newly lanched sour cream. Our sour cream is the prefect blend of sourness and creaminess that it can make any food instantly much better. It taste mild and does not overpower the original taste of a food, yet simply enchances it with its sour goodness. Enjoy it with biscuits, bread and even rice.
